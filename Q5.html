Colle dell'Agnello

27th May, 2016
Stage 19
Pinerolo to Risoul

It's a cliche, but is a cliche for a reason, and it was proven true Friday on Stage 19 of the Giro d'Italia, where the general classification was completely upended in one of the most dramatic days of Grand Tour racing in recent memory. At a Giro that seemed to have its GC winner decided with only three stages remaining, a mountain stage that included the climb over the Colle dell'Agnello, this year's highest pass at 2744 metres, and the uphill finish at Risoul in France, changed everything. (CyclingTips)
